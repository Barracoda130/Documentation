## Text formatting
**Use ** around text ** to make it bold**
*Use * around text * or _around text_ to make it italic*

### Lists
Use - to make normally bullet pointed list and use 1. to make numbered lists.
For a checklist type '- [ ]' (dash space [ space ])

- unordered item
- unordered item

1. ordered list
2. item 2

- [ ] item 1
- [x] item 2
